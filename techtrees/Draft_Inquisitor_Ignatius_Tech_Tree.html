Commander Tech Tree - Lord Inquisitor Ignatius

Commander Affiliation Rule - Zealous Authority

"Lord Inquisitor Ignatius prides himself on his unflinching resolve and determination to purge the heretic, the mutant and most of all the Daemon from the Empire. His brazen attitude, flaming conviction and unrelenting ferocity have drawn many acolytes and kindred spirits to his banner. Even some who themselves would arise doubt about their determination towards the Emperor of Mankind find a place aboard the Ignatius' flagship, the Ardor. As long as they search for redemption with overzealous use of hammer and bolter and yield their resources to his sole cause, to his highest authority, Ignatius averts his wrath and denounces all outside claims of heresy that might befall his most useful of henchmen.

This uneasy web of allegiances and destructive retaliation against any outsiders daring to doubt Ignatius' path, has proven useful in securing influential allies, single-minded warriors and instilling dread in the hearts of any foe. Human and un-human alike."

In-Game Effect

All units with this commander affiliation gain the following:

Zealous Determination (aura) - Enemy units that fail a Battle-shock Test within 12 inches of a Character unit with this battle trait suffer D3+1 Mortal Wounds. Enemy units within 12 inches of a unit with this battle trait cannot auto-pass or ignore Battle-shock tests, excluding using the Insane Bravery Stratagem. Enemy units within 12 inches cannot improve their Leadership characteristic in any way.

Designer's commentary: auras do not stack. Rules that ignore another army's battle ability are really uncool.

Lore description:

Within the veiled chambers of the Ordo's Classified Armoury, a trove of forbidden knowledge and alien technology awaits. Ignatius, with a discerning eye, selects esoteric weapons that blur the lines between science and sorcery. Each piece, whether it whispers with the cutting grace of Aeldari shuriken technology, hums with the raw power of Kyn plasma, or shrouds its bearer in a protective Necrodermis cloak, is a relic of wars past and a harbinger of victories to come. These armaments, bestowed upon his chosen champions, are not merely tools of war; they are symbols of the Inquisitor's relentless pursuit of supremacy over the galaxy's darkest threats. In the hands of his acolytes, they become extensions of the Imperium's will, striking with precision and ferocity that transcends the mortal realm.
