Commander Tech Tree - Renegade Leader Krall

Commander Affiliation Rule - Watch your step!

In-Game Effect

Armies following RENEGADE LEADER KRALL have access to the “A Deadly Prize” stratagem.


A Deadly Prize (1CP)
TARGET: One INFANTRY or MOUNTED unit from your army within range of an objective marker you control.
WHEN: Start of the Command Phase.
EFFECT: That objective marker is said to be Sabotaged, and remains under your control even if you have no models within range of it, until your opponent controls it at the start or end of any turn. While an objective marker is Sabotaged and under your control, each time an enemy unit ends a Normal, Advance, Fall Back or Charge move within range of that objective marker, roll one D6: on a 2+, that enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds.
If said army already had access to this stratagem, once per game, it costs 0 CP.

In addition, the MINEFIELDS special rule is always in effect (i.e. on an advance roll of a 6, the unit takes 1 mortal wound), but only for the opposing force (unless otherwise specified).




Tech Tree Rules

  • Renegade Leader Krall has 2 battle trees and 1 map tree.
  • Click on the selection box of ear ability to see more details.
  • Within each branch, you can only pick one battle trait per tier.
  • Finally Renegade Leader Krall gives access to three distinct buildings to construct.

Rebels

Hordes, Explosions, Coordination ... what is not to love?

Tier 1

Lore description:

Rebellions are no one man job.


In-Game Effect:

When the enemy fires a weapon with the BLAST keyword at a friendly INFANTRY, SWARM or BEAST unit, reduce their number of attacks by 1 (to a minimum of 1).
Once per game, you may use the GO TO GROUND Stratagem for 0 CP, even if another unit from your army has already been targeted by that Stratagem this phase.
Gain access to the GRENADES stratagem.

Tier 2

Lore description:

Literally.


In-Game Effect:

Choose one terrain feature outside of any deployment zone. Do not let your opponent know which terrain feature this is. The first time an enemy unit ends a move on said terrain feature, you may immediately resolve the effects of the GRENADES stratagem on that unit for free, disregarding any other restrictions such as range or times the stratagem was used. After the first time this happens, the terrain feature no longer contains any mines and is safe to enter by the enemy.

If there are no terrain features on the board (or none that are accessible to enemy models), you may instead place a marker in no man’s land. The same effects apply the first time an enemy model starts or ends a move within 6” of that marker.

Lore description:

It is just a package, don't worry about it!


In-Game Effect:

Friendly units in your army firing weapons with the BLAST keyword increase their number of attacks with said weapons by 1.

When using the GRENADES stratagem, increase the number of D6’s rolled by 2.

Lore description:

Lead From the Front!


In-Game Effect:

Units that have CHARACTER models attached may add one to their BATTLESHOCK and DESPERATE ESCAPE tests.

In addition, your WARLORDS’ unit may still use stratagems when BATTLESHOCKED.

Tier 3

Lore description:

Gotta have each other's back!


In-Game Effect:

When using the OVERWATCH stratagem, select one friendly unit within 6” of your unit and within 24” of the targeted enemy unit. That friendly unit may also use the OVERWATCH stratagem at the same target for free.

Lore description:

They rely on their heavy armour. Show them their mistake.

Choose two enemy VEHICLE or MONSTER units or one TITANIC unit (excluding AIRCRAFT or those with the FLY keyword) at the start of the game.

Lore description:

Use what you can to keep yourself alive.


In-Game Effect:

INFANTRY, SWARM and BEAST models always have the Benefit of Cover when in your deployment zone, irrespective of any enemy ability.

INFANTRY models in your army gain the HEAVY keyword whilst in your deployment zone. If they fire a weapon that already had that keyword, they maintain the effects even if they move, as long as they stay within your deployment zone.

Tier 4

Lore description:

This is what elevates us from a mob to soldiers.


In-Game Effect:

Once per battle round, one CHARACTER model in your army may use their special ability on a unit they are not attached to that is within 12 inches of that CHARACTERS’ UNIT.

Example: A Space Marine Librarian may use their Psychic Fortress ability on an INFANTRY unit within 12 inches of the unit he is attached to. This special ability MUST be an ability that would have afflicted the entire unit, not just the CHARACTER model itself (i.e. Finest Hour, Refuse to Yield, Abundance of Sickness, etc.). This ability can only be used on a unit with the same main keyword (i.e. INFANTRY, VEHICLE, etc.) as the unit that CHARACTER is attached to (or can attach to), unless said ability specifically states the type of unit it can be used on.

Restriction:
You must have the 'Direct Command' skill (tier 2) to be able to use this skill.

Lore description:

Warranty’s just expired too.


In-Game Effect:

Once per game, you may choose to activate this ability when an enemy VEHICLE is about to fire. That units’ weapons gain the HAZARDOUS keyword, and they must take a hazardous test for every weapon they are equipped with, even if they will not fire it. They must also add 1 to the dice roll when making their HAZARDOUS tests for that enemy VEHICLE unit.

In addition, enemy units with the DEADLY DEMISE special rule add 1 to the roll made for that rule.

Lore description:

No backwards step.


In-Game Effect:

Once per game, when a friendly unit is being charged by an enemy unit, you may overwatch for no cost with said unit at full ballistic skill. In addition, you may use the GRENADES stratagem on that charging unit as well.

You may use this skill in combination with the Covering Fire skill, but only one unit may fire at full ballistic skill.

At the start your opponents’ first Command Phase, choose one objective marker in no man’s land. All units in your army may reroll wound rolls of 1 versus enemy units within range of that objective marker.

If there are no objective markers on the battlefield, you may either choose one terrain feature in no man’s land (in this case, you may reroll 1s to wound versus enemy units within said terrain feature) or the area within 6” of the centre of the battlefield.

Switch!

Once per game, when an enemy unit moves within 9 inches of a friendly unit (excluding VEHICLE units), that friendly unit may immediately Switch: They make a normal move of up to 6 inches away from the enemy unit.

A different friendly unit (excluding VEHICLE units) that were within 12” of the original position of the Switching unit may move up to the original position of the Switching unit, but does not have to. If they choose to do so, that unit gains +1 to their armour saving throws until the start of your next command phase.

Lore description:

Now you see me ...


In-Game Effect:

Your WARLORDS’ unit has the LONE OPERATIVE special rule until the first time they come into engagement range of an enemy unit.

Tier 6

Lore description:

We are the man behind you


In-Game Effect:

Once per game, when a friendly unit (excluding VEHICLE, MONSTER or TITANIC units and CHARACTER models) is removed from the battlefield as a casualty, place that unit in Reserves instead. (If they can DEEPSTRIKE through whatever means, they may do so.) This unit is treated as a brand new unit with exactly the same weapons and stats as the one it replaced (excluding any upgrades received through the Mistmeadows crusade special rules).

If you choose to use this ability on a BATTLELINE unit, you may use this ability twice, but only on BATTLELINE units. (These do not need to have been destroyed in the same phase, turn or battle round.)

Lore description:

Time to pull out all the stops.


In-Game Effect:

- BLAST weapons may gain the HAZARDOUS keyword.

- You may use the GRENADES stratagem as many times per turn as you want, and the first two times you use it, it costs 0 CP.
- You may use the Sabotaged Mechanics skill once per battle round instead of once per game.

Battle-Map Effect:

Once per week, select one BATTLELINE unit in your army. This unit permanently gains the following ability:
Field Medic: At the start of your command phase, heal D3 wounds. If all models in that unit have their starting number of wounds, but that unit is not at its Starting Strength, one destroyed model is returned to that unit with one wound remaining.

Example: A unit of Intercessors (which have a Wounds characteristic of 2) activates the Field Medic ability. The unit had a Starting Strength of 5, but currently contains 2 models, and one of those models has lost 1 wound. A 3 is rolled to see how many wounds are healed. The first of these healed wounds restores the wounded Intercessor back to 2 wounds. The second of these healed wounds returns a destroyed Intercessor to the battlefield with 1 wound remaining. The third of these healed wounds restores one of the remaining lost wounds to the same Intercessor that was just returned. The unit now contains 3 models each with a full 2 wounds.

If models in that unit have a wounds characteristic of 1, they may regain up to three models with this rule.

In case the selected unit has a similar rule, such as Reanimation Protocols, Death Korps Medi-pack or Cult Icon, increase the number of wounds restored by 1 instead.
